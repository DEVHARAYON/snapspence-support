FAQs

I can’t restore my subscription / premium features.

In the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re signed in to the same Apple ID used to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → select SnapSpence → Cancel.

How do refunds work?

Apple handles billing and refunds for App Store purchases.

My expenses didn’t sync / data is missing.

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If it persists, email us with details.
